LBN was honored to welcome roasting experts and technical partners for a visit, exchange, and **hands-on training at our factory**.

The working session focused on real-world experience with LBN roasting machines: from mechanical structure, airflow and heat transfer, to operating procedures and fine-tuning roast profiles for specific flavor objectives.

## About the Training Session

We believe that a great roasting machine comes not only from design and materials, but is also perfected through real-world testing, listening to feedback, and sharing knowledge with industry practitioners.

LBN always opens its doors to connect, learn, and work alongside the coffee community, aiming to deliver roasting solutions that are stable, efficient, and suited to real production needs.
