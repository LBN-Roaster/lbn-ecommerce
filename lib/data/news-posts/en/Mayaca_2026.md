LBN was honored to welcome **MAYACA DA NANG** – Tiến Mayaca for a visit and working session at our factory.

The connection between those who share a passion for coffee is the foundation for sustainable value and long-term growth.

## About the Working Session

The meeting was an opportunity for both parties to discuss production processes, share practical experience, and explore future partnership opportunities. LBN always values partnerships built on a foundation of passion and trust.
