LBN was proud to participate in the **Best of Robusta Gia Lai 2026** cupping event held at Stone Village Lab — a gathering of the finest high-quality Robusta samples from reputable producers.

This was not just a journey of flavor exploration, but also an opportunity to connect, learn, and promote the value of Vietnamese coffee to the world.

## About the Event

Best of Robusta Gia Lai is an annual Robusta coffee quality competition, bringing together roasters, experts, and farmers from across the Central Highlands. Each coffee sample is evaluated according to international standards for aroma, flavor, balance, and aftertaste.

## LBN and the Coffee Journey

As a professional coffee roasting machine manufacturer, LBN always works alongside roasters to elevate product quality. Participating in events like Best of Robusta helps us gain deeper understanding of real market needs and continue improving our roasting machine technology.
