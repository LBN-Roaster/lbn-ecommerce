LBN was truly honored to welcome the delegation from **Delta Hardware Industry Co., Ltd (DHI)** for a visit and working session at our production facility.

During the meeting, both parties had the opportunity to exchange insights on production processes, technical standards, and future development directions.

## About the Working Session

This was not only an occasion to showcase LBN's capabilities in furniture, stainless steel, and coffee roasting machines, but also a starting point for sustainable partnership opportunities in the future.

LBN firmly believes that meaningful connections and the sharing of practical value form the foundation for long-lasting partnerships.
