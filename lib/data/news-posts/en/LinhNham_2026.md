LBN was honored to partner with **Linh Nham Agricultural Cooperative** in the acceptance testing of a 30KG coffee roasting machine.

## About the Acceptance Testing

The 30KG coffee roasting machine was designed and manufactured at the LBN factory, meeting the following criteria:

- Robust design, suitable for professional operation
- Optimized roasting performance with high stability
- Meeting the demands of high-quality coffee production
- Supporting the journey to elevate Vietnamese coffee value
