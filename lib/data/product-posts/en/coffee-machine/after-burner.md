## Introduction

The LBN Afterburner processes smoke generated during coffee roasting by completely combusting it at 600–800°C, eliminating odors and harmful gases, ensuring a clean and safe working environment.

## Key Features

- Complete smoke combustion at 600–800°C
- Compatible with 6–60kg roasters
- Automatic control system, energy-efficient
- Minimizes environmental pollution
