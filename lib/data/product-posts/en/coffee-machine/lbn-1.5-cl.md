### Introduction

Proudly manufactured in Vietnam, the Classic Roaster is a product that combines precision mechanical engineering with deep coffee expertise.
LBN creates machines that are not only beautiful but also durable, accurate, and reliable. A skilled roaster needs a trustworthy "partner." The Classic Roaster is exactly that.

### Advantages:

- Easy heat and airflow control
- High-precision sensors
- Roasting drum optimized for heat exchange
- Suitable for coffee shops, workshops, and professional roasting facilities

### Warranty

24-month official warranty, with on-site technical support nationwide.

### Technical Specifications

| Specification       | Value                                                  |
| ------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------ |
| Roasting Capacity   | 1.5 kg/batch                                           |
| Roasting Time       | 8 – 12 min/batch                                       |
| Throughput          | 7.5 – 11 kg/h                                          |
| Dimensions (LxWxH)  | ≈ 1566 x 800 x 1730 mm                                 |
| Weight              | ~ 240 kg                                               |
| Control Mode        | Manual                                                 |
| Gas Type            | LPG / LNG / Natural                                    |
| Electrical Power    | 420 W – 1 phase 220V 50/60Hz                           |
| Roasting Profiles   | Save 20 profiles (temperature, drum speed, airflow...) |
| Full Automation     | Option: run 100% according to saved profiles           |
| Connectivity        | Wireless, compatible with Artisan software             |
| Cooling Time        | < 5 min                                                |
| Filtration System   | Cyclone for roasting drum                              |
| Power Outage Backup | Manual drum rotation via hand crank                    |

### Control & Electrical Components

- PLC Mitsubishi – Japan
- Touchscreen Mitsubishi – Japan
- Inverter Mitsubishi – Japan
- Temperature Module Autonics – South Korea
- Switching & Protection Devices Schneider (France), Siemens (Germany)
- Temperature Sensors Omron and Misumi – Japan
- Differential Pressure Gauge Magnehelic – China

### Motors

- Drum Motor: 90W – Transtecno – Italy
- Exhaust Motor: 90W – Enertech – Australia
- Cooling Motor: 180W – Enertech – Australia
- Stirring Motor: 60W – Leonhard – Taiwan

### Burner System

- Bar burner
- Controlled by SIGMA valve – Italy
- Input and output pressure gauges

### Structure & Materials

- Double-layer high-strength steel roasting drum
- Electrostatic powder-coated steel body with thermal insulation layer
- Hopper, cooling tray, and components made of stainless steel
- Heat-resistant glass viewing windows for beans and flame
