### Introduction

Proudly manufactured in Vietnam, the Modern Roaster is a product that combines mechanical engineering expertise with deep knowledge of coffee.
LBN creates machines that are not only beautiful but also durable, precise, and accurate. A skilled roaster needs a reliable "partner." The Modern Roaster is exactly that.

### Advantages :

- Easy heat & airflow control
- High-precision sensors
- Roasting drum optimized for heat exchange
- Suitable for coffee shops, workshops, and professional roasting facilities

### Warranty

24-month official warranty with nationwide on-site technical support.

### Technical Specifications

| Specification      | Value                                                            |
| ------------------ | ---------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Roasting capacity  | 6 kg/batch                                                       |
| Roasting time      | 10 - 20 min/batch                                                |
| Throughput         | 18 - 36 kg/h                                                     |
| Dimensions (LxWxH) | ≈ 2034 x 1258 x 2128 mm                                          |
| Weight             | ~ 485 kg                                                         |
| Control mode       | Automatic/Manual                                                 |
| Gas type           | LPG / LNG / Natural                                              |
| Electrical power   | 1.56 KW – 1 phase 220V 50/60Hz                                   |
| Roasting profiles  | Save 20 profiles (temperature, drum speed, airflow…)             |
| Fully automatic    | Option: runs 100% according to saved profile                     |
| Connectivity       | Wireless, compatible with Artisan software                       |
| Cooling time       | < 5 min                                                          |
| Filtration system  | Cyclone for roasting drum with Airlock automatic chaff discharge |

### Control & Electrical Components

- PLC Delta - Taiwan
- Touchscreen Delta - Taiwan
- Inverter Delta - Taiwan or equivalent
- Temperature module Autonics - South Korea
- Switching and protection devices Siemens - Germany
- Temperature sensors Omron and Misumi - Japan
- Pneumatic system: cylinders, air filters... SMC - Japan.

### Motors

- Drum motor: 0.37 KW - Transtecno - Italy
- Exhaust motor: 0.55 KW - Enertech - Australia
- Cooling motor: 0.37 KW - Enertech - Australia
- Stirring motor: 0.18 KW - Transtecno - Italy
- Airlock motor: 0.09 KW - Transtecno - Italy

### Burner System

- Bar burner
- Controlled by SIGMA valve - Italy
- Inlet and outlet pressure gauges.

### Structure & Materials

- Roasting drum designed with 2 layers of high-strength steel.
- Machine body in electrostatic powder-coated steel with thermal insulation.
- Hopper, cooling tray, insulating jacket, and other components in stainless steel
- Coffee bean and flame observation windows made of high-temperature resistant glass.
