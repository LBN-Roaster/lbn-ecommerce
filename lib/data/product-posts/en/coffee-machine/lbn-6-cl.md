### Introduction

Proudly manufactured in Vietnam, the Classic Roaster is a product that combines mechanical engineering expertise with deep knowledge of coffee.
LBN creates machines that are not only beautiful but also durable, precise, and accurate. A skilled roaster needs a reliable "partner." The Classic Roaster is exactly that.

### Advantages :

- Easy heat & airflow control
- High-precision sensors
- Roasting drum optimized for heat exchange
- Suitable for coffee shops, workshops, and professional roasting facilities

### Warranty

24-month official warranty with nationwide on-site technical support.

### Control & Electrical Components

- PLC Mitsubishi – Japan
- Touchscreen Mitsubishi – Japan
- Inverter Mitsubishi – Japan
- Temperature module Autonics – South Korea
- Switching & protection devices Schneider (France), Siemens (Germany)
- Temperature sensors Omron and Misumi – Japan
- Pneumatic system: cylinders, air filters… SMC – Japan

### Motors

- Drum motor: 0.37 KW – Transtecno – Italy
- Exhaust motor: 0.55 KW – Enertech – Australia
- Cooling motor: 0.37 KW – Enertech – Australia
- Stirring motor: 0.18 KW – Transtecno – Italy
- Airlock motor: 0.09 KW – Transtecno – Italy

### Burner System

- Bar burner
- Controlled by SIGMA valve – Italy
- Inlet and outlet pressure gauges

### Structure & Materials

- Roasting drum with 2 layers of high-strength steel
- Machine body in electrostatic powder-coated steel with thermal insulation
- Hopper, cooling tray, insulating jacket, and components in stainless steel
- Coffee bean and flame observation windows made of high-temperature resistant glass
