### Introduction

Proudly manufactured in Vietnam, the MAROSA Roaster is a product that combines mechanical engineering expertise with deep knowledge of coffee.
LBN creates machines that are not only beautiful but also durable, precise, and accurate. A skilled roaster needs a reliable "partner." The MAROSA Roaster is exactly that.

### Advantages:

- Easy heat and airflow control
- High-precision sensors
- Roasting drum optimized for heat exchange
- Suitable for coffee shops, workshops, and professional roasting facilities

### Warranty

24-month official warranty with nationwide on-site technical support.

- Bar burner

- Roasting drum designed with dual-layer high-strength steel.
