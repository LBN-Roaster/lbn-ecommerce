## Introduction

The LBN **Airlock** coffee roaster is designed for professional roasters, featuring a highly precise temperature control system that delivers consistent and uniform roast batches.

## Key Features

- Temperature control system accurate to ±1°C
- Premium 304 stainless steel roasting chamber, resistant to high temperatures
- Rapid cooling system after roasting
- Intuitive and user-friendly control interface
- Suitable for 6–15kg and 30–60kg batch capacities
