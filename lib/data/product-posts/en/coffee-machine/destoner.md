## Introduction

The LBN Destoner uses powerful airflow to remove stones, impurities, and defective beans from green coffee before roasting, protecting the roaster and ensuring batch quality.

## Key Features

- Efficiently separates stones and impurities using high-pressure airflow
- 304 stainless steel body, durable and easy to clean
- Compact design, easy to install
- Low power consumption

## Warranty

24-month official warranty.
