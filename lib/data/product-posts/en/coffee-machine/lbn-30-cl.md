### Introduction

Proudly manufactured in Vietnam, the Classic Roaster is a product that combines mechanical engineering expertise with deep knowledge of coffee.
LBN creates machines that are not only beautiful but also durable, precise, and accurate. A skilled roaster needs a reliable "partner." The Classic Roaster is exactly that.

### Advantages:

- Easy heat and airflow control
- High-precision sensors
- Roasting drum optimized for heat exchange
- Suitable for coffee shops, workshops, and professional roasting facilities

### Warranty

24-month official warranty with nationwide on-site technical support.

- PLC Mitsubishi – Japan
- Mitsubishi touchscreen – Japan

- Siemens switching & protection devices – Germany

- Pneumatic system: cylinders, air filters… SMC – Japan

### Motors

- Roasting drum motor: 1.5 KW – Transtecno – Italy
- Smoke exhaust motor: 1.5 KW – Enertech – Australia
- Cooling motor: 1.5 KW – Enertech – Australia
- Stirring motor: 0.18 KW – Transtecno – Italy
- Airlock motor: 0.18 KW – Transtecno – Italy

### Burner System

**Option 1 – Bar Burner**

- Bar burner
- Controlled by SIGMA valve – Italy
- Input and output pressure gauges

**Option 2 – Gas Burner**

- Ecoflam gas burner – Italy
- Controlled by SIGMA valve – Italy
- Dungs gas valve assembly – Germany
- Input and output pressure gauges

### Structure & Materials

- Roasting drum designed with dual-layer high-strength steel
- Machine body made of electrostatic powder-coated steel with thermal insulation layer
- Hopper, cooling tray, insulation casing, and other components made of stainless steel
- Coffee bean observation window and flame observation window made of high-temperature resistant glass
