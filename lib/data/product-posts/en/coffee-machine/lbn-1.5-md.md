### Introduction

Proudly manufactured in Vietnam, the Modern Roaster is a product that combines precision mechanical engineering with deep coffee expertise.
LBN creates machines that are not only beautiful but also durable, accurate, and reliable. A skilled roaster needs a trustworthy "partner." The Modern Roaster is exactly that.

### Advantages:

- Easy heat and airflow control
- High-precision sensors
- Roasting drum optimized for heat exchange
- Suitable for coffee shops, workshops, and professional roasting facilities

### Warranty

24-month official warranty, with on-site technical support nationwide.
